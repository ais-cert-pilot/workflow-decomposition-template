# Project Constraints

## Budget
- **$2,000/month** for AI tooling (all-in: software, APIs, integrations)
- No additional headcount budget
- Derek Osei controls the budget and must approve any spending

## Timeline
- **30 days** to show visible results
- Laura wants quick wins that demonstrate value to Maria

## People
- **Maria Ruiz is resistant.** She told Laura: "I don't trust a robot to talk to someone who just got hit by a truck." Any plan that touches client interactions must account for Maria's buy-in or it will fail in practice.
- **Derek Osei is compliance-first.** He will ask where every piece of data goes. He will not approve anything that puts the firm at legal risk.
- **Laura is the decision-maker** but she will not override Maria on client-facing changes without Maria's agreement.

## Compliance
- **Attorney-client privilege:** All communications between the firm and clients are legally protected. Data cannot be stored in systems that violate this privilege.
- **Bar association rules:** Conflicts of interest must be caught. Failure to identify a conflict can result in sanctions, malpractice liability, or disbarment.
- **Medical records (HIPAA-adjacent):** Client medical information must be handled with appropriate safeguards.
- **Retainer agreements:** Must be properly executed. The client relationship is not established until the retainer is signed and Laura accepts the case.

## Existing Systems
- Clio (case management) is the system of record. Any AI must integrate with or complement Clio, not replace it.
- DocuSign is working well and should not be changed.
