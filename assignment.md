# Project: Workflow Decomposition
## AIS Certification — AI Consultant Tier

You just finished a discovery call with Laura Henderson, managing partner of Henderson & Associates, a personal injury law firm. She walked you through their entire client intake process.

Your job: extract every step from the transcript and build a process map that shows what should be automated and what should stay human.

---

## Your Deliverable

Save your process map to `deliverables/process-map.md` as a markdown table with these columns:

| # | Step | Automate / Keep Human / Hybrid | Justification |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | [step description] | [your label] | [one sentence: why] |
| 2 | ... | ... | ... |

**Be thorough.** Steps buried in tangents and asides count. The transcript is messy on purpose — real discovery calls are messy.

**Labels:**
- **Automate** — AI handles this end-to-end, no human needed
- **Keep Human** — this stays with a person
- **Hybrid** — AI assists but a human is still involved

**Justifications:** One sentence per step. Explain *why* this step should be automated, kept human, or handled as a hybrid. Consider the firm's constraints (budget, compliance, team dynamics) in your reasoning.
